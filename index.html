Story

Subject M will try to figure out what's going on and try to escape from this facility. While escaping from the facility, he learns about himself in a document belonging to the "HELL PROJECT" and realizes that he has some superhuman abilities and uses these abilities to escape from the facility over time. The story will take shape according to the player's choices.
